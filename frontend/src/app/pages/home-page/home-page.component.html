Looking for your next roommate?

We can help! Scroll through our extensive library of posts with open beds to fill or create your own post and let people come to you. No matter whether you already have a place, or want to move into some place new, Roommated has your back.

Find the perfect location!

Our interactive map page allows you to find posts near you or anywhere in the world! You can also apply filtering to only show posts that meet your specific needs.

Group up with your friends!

Don't go it alone if you don't have to. Group up with your friends or current roommates and start making or applying to posts together.


Don't have a group to start your search with? No problem! With Roommated you can start by creating a group of 1 and merge with another group later.

How it works?

1.

Create your group

Create a group with an awesome name and invite your friends! Any friend you invite will need to create their own account so make sure they do that before you start inviting. Starting your search alone? No worries, groups of one work great as well.

2.

Apply or make a post

Once you have your group setup, its time to start making and applying to posts. If you looking to fill a vacancy at your current place of residence, making a post is the way to go. If you are looking to move to a new area, applying is your best option. Lease is almost up? You may find yourself making and applying to posts. Every group is different so do what fits yours best.

3.

Manage your applications

Finally comes time to manage your applications. If you have made a post, you have the option of accepting or denying applications. Accepted applications allow the applying group and posting group to communicate and interact within site further. An accepted application doesn't mean your groups are officially roommates obviously, but it is a great first step!
